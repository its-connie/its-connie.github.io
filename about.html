About Me

Biography

Connie is a designer based in New York City. In her free time, you’ll find her noodling away on weird instruments, thinking and writing about sociotechnical systems, or bopping around the City as an amateur public space critic. She loves a good side quest and has strong opinions about how they make us more human. Ask her about it sometime!

My Mission

My mission is to create digital experiences that are inclusive, performant, and delightful. I aim to solve real user problems with pragmatic design and engineering.
